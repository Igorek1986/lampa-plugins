🎬 NUMParser plugin for Lampa  
A powerful source of up-to-date content with convenient filtering and flexible settings!

🔹 Key features:

- Huge catalog of movies, series and cartoons  
- Automatic filtering of already watched content  
- Hides movies/series once a configurable progress is reached. Requires the [TimecodeUser](https://github.com/Igorek1986/lampa-plugins/tree/main/module/TimecodeUser) module  
- Flexible category display settings  
- 4K support and fresh theatrical releases  
- Integration with Lampa watch history  

🗂 Content categories:

- New releases (including Russian films)  
- Series and animated series  
- 4K-quality content  
- Anime and cartoons  
- Movies by release year (from 1980 to the current year)  
- Top picks  

🆕 What's new:

- **Category order** — a "Category order" button with drag-and-drop: reorder and hide any categories to your taste.  
- **Genre / actor / director collections** — automatic collections (comedies, action, thrillers, horror, sci-fi, etc.), reshuffled each time.  
- **Age rating on cards** — colored 0+/6+/12+/16+/18+ badges in the poster corner. Setting: "Age rating on cards".  
- **Quality format** — choose how quality is shown: full (WEBDL 1080p, BDRip…) or simplified (SD / 720p / 1080p / 4K).  
- **Hide unrated** — a separate toggle to hide content without age marking.  
- **Bind device by code** — no need to copy a token manually anymore: an activation code is shown and entered on the website ("My devices → Bind device"). Manual token entry is still available.  
- **View tracking for "Popular"** — the "Popular" section is built from your views; data is sent automatically, without device binding.  

⚙️ Convenient settings:

- Watch threshold setting (50-100%)  
- Enabling/disabling and ordering categories  
- Option to hide watched and unrated content  
- Changing the source name in the menu  
- Video quality display format  
- Multi-language support (via TMDB settings)  

🔧 Technical highlights:

- Optimized content loading  
- Episode cards support for series  
- Integration with Lampa favorites  
- Automatic list updates  
- The plugin is ideal for those who always want access to up-to-date content with convenient filtering and personalized display.
